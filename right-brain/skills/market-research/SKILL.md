# Market Research Skill

## Research Standards

1. Every claim needs a source
2. Prefer recent data, flag stale data
3. Include contrarian evidence
4. Translate findings into decisions
5. Separate fact, inference, recommendation

## Research Modes

### Market Sizing
- Top-down estimates from reports
- Bottom-up from realistic customer assumptions
- Explicit assumptions for every leap

### Competitive Analysis
- Product reality, not marketing copy
- Funding and investor history
- Traction metrics if public
- Strengths, weaknesses, positioning gaps

### Technology Scan
- How it works (architecture)
- Trade-offs and adoption signals
- Integration complexity
- Lock-in, security, compliance risk

## Output Format

1. Executive summary
2. Key findings
3. Implications
4. Risks and caveats
5. Recommendation
6. Sources

## Quality Gate

- All numbers sourced or labeled estimates
- Old data flagged
- Recommendation follows from evidence
- Risks included
- Output makes a decision easier
