# Creative Principles — Right Brain

## Design Thinking

1. **Empathize** — Understand the user's real problem
2. **Define** — Frame the problem clearly
3. **Ideate** — Generate multiple solutions
4. **Prototype** — Build the smallest thing that tests the idea
5. **Test** — Validate with real feedback

## Communication Standards

- Lead with the conclusion, not the process
- Use visuals (diagrams, tables) over walls of text
- Tailor language to the audience (technical vs business)
- Include actionable next steps in every deliverable

## Research Quality

- Every claim needs a source
- Separate fact from inference from recommendation
- Include contrarian views and downside cases
- Make research decision-oriented, not encyclopedic

## Architecture Standards

- Document trade-offs, not just decisions
- Consider the 3 horizons: now, next quarter, next year
- Prefer boring technology for critical paths
- Design for the team you have, not the team you want
