# Research Command

Invoke the **researcher** agent to conduct multi-source research on $ARGUMENTS.

## What This Does

1. Search across multiple sources (web, social, technical)
2. Rank results by engagement and credibility
3. Include contrarian evidence and risk factors
4. Deliver a decision-oriented brief

## Output Format

1. **Executive Summary** — 2-3 sentence answer
2. **Key Findings** — top 3-5 insights ranked by signal strength
3. **Evidence** — sources, data points, expert opinions
4. **Risks & Counterarguments** — what could be wrong
5. **Recommendation** — what to do based on evidence
6. **Sources** — links and attributions

## Quality Standards

- Every claim sourced or labeled as estimate
- Recent data preferred, stale data flagged
- Contrarian views included
- Output makes a decision easier
